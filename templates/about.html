About

BrasBasahBooks

Basah Stair well   By Bras Basah Complex

Bras Basah Complex is known as City of Books, a mall that is well known for its huge collection of bookstores. Since the 1980s, this place has allowed many book merchants to come together to make book sales. It has since become a cultural heritage for arts and cultures.

With the onset of Covid-19, many businesses have seen a decline in physical sales. These are due to various reasons such as less customers shopping physically due to the fear of contracting the virus, ease of online shopping and more.

As Singaporeans vote with their wallet and convenience stores in Bras Basah Complex which did not manage to create a digital presence to adapt to the new sudden changes are facing a threat to their existence. The aim of this project is to create a digital presence for the book stores in Bras Basah Complex. The project will be a website that will allow users to browse the books in the store and also to buy the books online.

We hope you will find a book in the store that will be interesting in this website.

Thank you for supporting this project of ours.
